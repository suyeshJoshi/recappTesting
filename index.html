Using Recapp

Learning is primarily done through observing and listening. Therefore, it is important to know what to focus on and what to take away from a topic.

Listening, Observing and Doing are some of the key methods of learning globally. The hope and dream of every learner is to master the Learning Mantra - Know what to focus on and Know what or how to say or write what you have learnt.

Introducing the Learning Droplet – The Audio based Unit of Learning supported by a set of Images and a Web-link of a video, audio, animation or graphic or a set of Step-by-Step Solution images.

There are 4 types of Droplets that we provide, which together cover nearly everything a student may need to study – Definitions, Explanations, Summary and Q&As.

There are 2 models of creating the Learning Solutions – The Standard or Direct Approach and the Progressive or Step-by-Step approach.

There is no limit to the range of academic or non-academic subjects that can be covered, no limitations on the language of instruction, no limits to the type of skills that you want to impart and no limits to the amount of content you want to create.

How to create
As a Teacher / Tutor / Trainer:

Every Learning Unit or Learning Droplet has a Header/ Question/ Intro and then the detailed Body content or answer follows.

As part of the preparation,there exists an option to click a picture or download one from the web, or write out a solution on paper and photograph it. Then decide if you want to include a link to a video oran interesting article. Upload the images and link and recordyour audio content.

You have an option of providing the answer directly in a single go or else do a progressive step-by-step explanation – here the image segment and audio explanation is provided for each step of the solution, enabling easy understanding of the answer progression.

Once done, just save, upload, and publish, every student assigned to you will receive it. So it’s a very simple process of Click, Speakand Publish

ReCapp is very intuitive and provides a powerful way to influence the learning process at home.

As a Student

If you belong to a school or institution that has subscribed, then you will have to choose from 2 options to record and store.

The default option is the Institution tab. You can supplement what the teacher has published. You can add your own Learning Unit or Droplet within the structure provided by the teacher.

The other option is to create Learning Units or Droplets under the “Own” tab and this is typically meant for content that’s not related to your institution. Here you create all the content you wish – subject, chapter and topics .

As a direct individual user

You create content under the “Own” tab. Here you create all the content you want – subject, chapter and topics.

As part of the preparation you can click a picture or download images from the web, or write out a solution on paper and photograph it or even alink to a video or an interesting article. Upload the images and link and then record your audio content.

You have the option to input the answer directly in a single go or else do a progressive step-by-step explanation – here the image segment and audio explanation is provided for each step of the solution, enabling easy understanding of the answer progression.

How to use it for learning

Learning is now available at your fingertips.Select what you want to learn and click.

No need to carry your books around, just plug in your earphones and start Learning – Anytime and Everywhere.

You have the option of selecting the Learning Units every time time you want to study, or, if you feel that you would definitely go back to a certain set of units, then create a Playlist that can be reused, edited or deleted at anytime.

You can select an entire Chapter or a Topic or group of Topics under a Chapter or a Droplet type within a Chapter or a bunch of Droplets under a Topic and study. In a Playlist you can add Units across Chapters.

There are 2 modes for Learning :

Revise Mode

With this option the entire content selected replays one after the other continuously. Revising for a test or exam is simplified.

Recap Mode

You simulate a test or exam environment here. The selected content gets randomized for replay - the header plays first and you attempt to answer the same on paper or state it out aloud and then play the body or answer and compare the two and score yourself on your performance.
